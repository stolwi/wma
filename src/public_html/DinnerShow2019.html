Williamsburg High School Dessert/Dinner Show 2019

Featuring Sound Attraction, Jazz Outlet, Sound Sensation and Jazz Works Saturday, April 13, 2019 1:00 Dessert Matinee Show - $12 5:30 Dinner Show - $22

The Williamsburg Music Association proudly invites parents of show choir/jazz band/combo students to make preferred reservations for the Dessert/Dinner Show, prior to the general public. This event is the main fundraiser for the Williamsburg Music Association. Reservations are required as all seats are assigned. NO RESERVATIONS will be made without full payment.

Order ONLINE by clicking here Make sure to provide the name of your student in the note as you check out from the online store.
If you would rather pay by check, fill out the form your student brought home. You may print it out another copy from here. Your completed form and payment should be delivered to the high school office.

Reservations for the general public begin on March 11th and table assignments are made based on the date of the reservation. Posters are available of the High School Ensembles for $7 each.

Please direct reservation questions to Jennifer Lane at jenniferlane@williamsburg.k12.ia.us
